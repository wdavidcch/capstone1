A quick look to our Story

The global chain that Little Caesars is today began with a blind date between Mike Ilitch and Marian Bayoff that was arranged by Mike’s father in 1954. Within just a matter of months, the couple was married.

2020

The Ilitches purchase a mushroom farm to improve mushroom quality and pricing. Little Caesars Mushroom Farms, Inc. ultimately expanded to become Little Caesars primary, full-service distribution company known today as Blue Line Foodservice Distribution.

2021

Little Caesars celebrates the opening of its 50th restaurant. It also went international with the opening of its first store in Canada. Today, the company has stores in all 50 states and 18 international markets.
